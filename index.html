About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate at Washington State University. My general research interests are in the field of the robustness of machine learning systems. My current research focuses on developing efficient learning and optimization algorithms to improve the reliability and safety of deep learning algorithms for diverse settings and data spaces. My current work includes:

  • Robust deep learning for the time-series domain with diverse applications including human activity recognition and mobile health.
  • Trustworthy machine learning for sequential data witha focus on Out-Of-Distribution detection.
  • Uncertainty quantification for reliable machine-learning using conformal prediction.
